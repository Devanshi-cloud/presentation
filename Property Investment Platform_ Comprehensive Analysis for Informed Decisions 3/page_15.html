Phased Go-to-Market Approach

Strategic implementation plan designed for controlled growth, continuous learning, and market adaptation

1
2
3

Phase 1: MVP & Early Adoption

6-9 Months

Geographic Focus

Launch in 1-2 key metropolitan areas (Mumbai, Bengaluru) with high real estate activity

Core Features

  • RERA project search & verification
  • Basic investment reports
  • Essential location analytics

Target Audience

Tech-savvy individual investors, first-time homebuyers

Phase 2: Feature Enhancement

9-18 Months

Geographic Focus

Expand to 3-5 additional major cities based on RERA data availability

Enhanced Features

  • Advanced financial modeling
  • Comprehensive location intelligence
  • Customizable reports & dashboards
  • Tiered subscription model

Target Audience

Serious investors, property consultants, smaller real estate firms

Phase 3: Scaling & Diversification

18+ Months

Geographic Focus

Comprehensive coverage of all major RERA-compliant states

New Offerings

  • Portfolio management tools
  • AI-powered investment recommendations
  • B2B solutions & enterprise integrations
  • Mobile application launch

Target Audience

Institutional investors, wealth management firms, enterprise clients

Marketing Evolution

  • Digital & content marketing
  • Direct outreach to communities
  • Free access for early adopters
  • Industry partnerships
  • Webinars & online workshops
  • Referral program & paid advertising
  • Thought leadership campaigns
  • Industry conference presence
  • Strategic financial alliances
