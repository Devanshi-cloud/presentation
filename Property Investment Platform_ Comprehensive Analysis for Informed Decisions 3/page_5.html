Informed Decisions

  • Multi-level investment management

Streamlined Analysis

  • Immediate access to value zone data
  • Align valuations with market realities

Real-World Application

Maharashtra is utilizing GIS mapping to bring clarity and accuracy to Ready Reckoner (RR) rates, the minimum property values set for transactions, moving from subjective estimations to data-driven, transparent valuations.
