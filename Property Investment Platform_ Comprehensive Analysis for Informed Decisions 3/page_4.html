The RERA Data Challenge

Importance of RERA Data

  • Enhances transparency, accountability, and consumer protection in real estate
  • Mandates developers to register projects and disclose critical details (layout, approvals, timelines)
  • Requires 70% of project funds to be deposited in escrow accounts, ensuring proper fund utilization
  • Protects homebuyers' interests and boosts confidence in investment decisions

Technical Access Challenges

  • Each Indian state operates its own RERA portal with distinct formats, search interfaces, and data structures
  • Lack of standardization makes cross-state data aggregation and analysis complex
  • Advanced anti-bot mechanisms and CAPTCHA-based roadblocks prevent automated data extraction
  • Difficulty detecting changes or newly added listings in real-time, crucial for timely decisions
